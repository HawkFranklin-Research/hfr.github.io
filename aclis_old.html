ACLIS — Adaptive Clinically Intelligent System

A unified interface for AI‑enabled devices, agents, and foundation models — with physician‑defined control.

Request Demo See the pillars

The challenge

Taming the AI revolution for healthcare

  • Tool overload across models, agents, and devices.
  • Integration gaps with clinical IT and native device UIs.
  • Regulatory obligations and hospital governance.
  • Training burden for clinicians and staff.
  • Fragmented outcomes and lack of auditability.

The solution

One surface. Protocol‑safe intelligence.

  • Single integration hub for devices, models, and agents.
  • Clinical AI Protocol for physician‑defined rules and oversight.
  • Agentic orchestration that learns from outcomes within guardrails.
  • Secure, in‑house alignment of foundation models.
  • Progressive adoption—learn one system; add tech without retraining.

Core pillars

How ACLIS works

Universal Integration Hub

One interface for devices, models, and agents; vendor‑neutral and able to wrap native UIs when needed.

Clinical AI Protocol

Rules for access, thresholds, alerts, and audit—defined by hospitals and physicians.

Agentic Intelligence

Outcome‑aware orchestration that improves safely within protocol constraints.

Safety & Governance

Audit logs, access controls, explainability hooks, and change control built‑in.

Deployment Options

On‑prem or private cloud; data‑local training where required by policy.

Interoperability

Standards‑friendly, vendor‑neutral design; integrates clinical IT and medical devices.
