04 | Solution Architecture

Google Derm Foundation + MIL

Unlike standard models that analyze single images, PelliScope utilizes Multiple Instance Learning (MIL) to aggregate evidence from close-up, angled, and contextual photos—mimicking the actual clinical workflow.
