Clinical AI Protocol in Action

Dr. Evans' Oncology Protocol

  • Regulatory Controls: Only approved AI agents may access imaging data. Confidence threshold for alerts set to >95%.
  • Physician Heuristics: "For my local diabetic population, flag HbA1c at 6.8%." This rule is now active for all my patients.
  • Model Alignment Request: "Fine-tune TabPFN on our internal cohort of patients with EGFR L858R mutation to improve prognosis accuracy."

Dr. Chen's Cardiology Protocol

  • Device Preference: Default to GE Vivid E95 for echocardiograms. Auto-load my 'Stress Echo' preset for relevant cases.
  • Alerting Threshold: Notify me if any patient's wearable device detects an AFib episode lasting longer than 5 minutes.
USP 3

Clinical AI Protocol

Complete regulatory power, transparency, and alignment over every AI-Enabled Decision.

  • Physician-Defined Control: Clinicians and administrators decide which AI agents are active, what data they access, and under what clinical parameters.
  • Assistive Intelligence: The system operates on physicians preference of AI's assistance level, includes long term phenotypic clinical notes & existing diagnostic outcomes.
  • Secure In-House Alignment: Fine-tune foundation models on local patient cohorts via the Copilot Trainer, without sensitive data ever leaving your firewall.
  • Dynamic & Proactive Interface: Dashboards are built on-the-fly based on doctor specialty and patient context, with active surveillance for emerging threats.
