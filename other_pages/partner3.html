Our Research Directions

Pioneering AI methodologies to tackle complex healthcare challenges

In-Context Learning for GNNs

Novel approaches enabling Graph Neural Networks to perform in-context learning, allowing adaptation to new graph structures with minimal examples. Inspired by Large Language Models, this is crucial for dynamic medical data scenarios.

Tabular Foundation Models

Developing robust foundation models specifically engineered for tabular data. By pre-training on diverse datasets, these models achieve superior zero-shot or few-shot performance in clinical prediction tasks.

Survival Foundation Models

Advancing specialized foundation models for survival analysis. These integrate multi-modal patient data to deliver more accurate prognostic predictions, contributing to personalized medicine strategies.

Medical Vision Models

Developing specialized computer vision architectures for radiology and pathology applications, with focus on interpretability and clinical workflow integration.
