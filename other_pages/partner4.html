Key Products and Innovations

ACLIS

Our flagship Adaptive Clinically Intelligent System, a unified platform for orchestrating AI agents, models, and medical devices under physician governance.

Machine Learning Copilot Agent (MLCA)

An LLM-powered framework that automates classical ML workflows for research, from data processing to reproducible reporting in cancer genomics.

DermAssist

A dermatology-focused vision model that supports clinicians in lesion triage and provides diagnostic assistance for skin conditions.

RadioAssist

A radiology copilot designed to automate and enhance imaging interpretation across X-ray, CT, and MRI pipelines for workflow support.

PathoLens (Pathology Copilot)

An AI system for whole-slide imaging that assists pathologists with quality assurance, triage, and pattern spotting in digital pathology.
