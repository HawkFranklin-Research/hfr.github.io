The Product: Clinically-Validated AI for Dermatology

PelliScope is an intelligent triage tool that provides differential condition detection for skin lesions, ready for deployment in telemedicine and pre-medical inquiry workflows.

Infectious vs. Non-Infectious Triage

Rapidly flags lesions to distinguish between infectious and non-infectious presentations for immediate triage.

10-Condition Differential Diagnosis

Advanced model provides probability assessments for 10 common dermatological conditions (Eczema, Psoriasis, Tinea, etc.).

Resource-Optimized & Accessible

Runs efficiently on standard CPU hardware (no GPU dependency), enabling deployment in low-resource settings. Ultra-low operational cost (≈$93/month for cloud hosting).
