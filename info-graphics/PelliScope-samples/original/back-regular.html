Our Philosophy

HawkFranklin Research was founded by a passionate, bootstrapped group of researchers and engineers. We believe the greatest barrier to innovation isn't a lack of ideas, but the R&D inequality between large corporations and everyone else.

"We are a resilient, self-funded team. We believe in our mission to build impactful technology and will succeed with or without external funding—this is an opportunity to join a team that is already moving forward."

Our Operational Model

Path A: Partner-Initiated R&D

You bring a defined challenge. We build the dedicated AI solution, acting as your external deep-tech R&D team.

Path B: HFR-Initiated Products

We identify a market gap, build a validated product (like PelliScope), and then seek commercial partners to scale it.
