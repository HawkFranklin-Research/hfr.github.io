PelliScope

AI for pre-medical dermatology inquiry

PelliScope is a clinical vision assistant that helps clinicians triage lesions prior to specialty referral. It distinguishes infectious vs. non-infectious presentations and guides users toward structured differential diagnosis workflows.

Key Capabilities

Free lesion check-ups to flag infectious vs. non-infectious presentations for rapid triage.

Paid differential diagnosis path for complex dermatological cases with clinician-ready context.

Resource-optimized executable that runs on CPU-grade hardware—no GPU dependency.

Clinical Intake Snapshot

Embed PelliScope within tele-derm portals or urgent-care kiosks to capture structured lesion evidence, images, and vitals before a clinician consult.

Modalities

RGB photos, metadata, symptom prompts

Latency

< 3 sec on Intel i5 class CPU

Deployment

Offline-ready, containerized

Output

Triage flag, diff-dx suggestions, PDF handoff
