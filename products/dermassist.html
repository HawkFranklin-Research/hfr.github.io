How DermAssist Works

Image Upload

Clinicians upload skin lesion images through an intuitive interface

AI Processing

CNN/Transformer pipelines analyze the image using dermatology datasets

Probability Assessment

System calculates likelihood scores for various conditions

Clinical Decision Support

Presents findings to assist (not replace) clinician diagnosis

Key Features

  • Lesion detection and classification
  • Risk scoring and triage recommendation
  • Dermatology-focused knowledge base
  • Explainable visual outputs

Clinical Value

  • Faster triage and workflow support
  • Augments, not replaces, clinician judgment
  • Potential to reduce unnecessary biopsies
  • Designed for safety and auditability

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DermAssist a diagnostic tool?

No. DermAssist is a clinical decision support tool designed to assist clinicians by providing assessments, not definitive diagnoses.

What skin conditions can it assess?

The system provides probabilistic assessments for common dermatological conditions such as eczema, psoriasis, and dermatitis.

Is patient data secure?

Yes. DermAssist is designed with strict privacy and security safeguards, supporting on-premise deployment and audit trails.

Can I try a demo?

We provide controlled demos for clinical partners. Please contact us to request access.
