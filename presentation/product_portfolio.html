MedDiscover

Biomedical RAG Copilot for Evidence-Grounded Q&A

MedDiscover transforms curated biomedical PDFs into an interactive research assistant. The Gradio front-end pairs MedCPT embeddings, FAISS retrieval, and GPT-4o/Ollama decoders so researchers can interrogate metabolomics and rare-disease literature with grounded, citation-aware answers.

Key Modules

  • Drag-and-drop PDF ingestion with 500-word chunking and persisted FAISS indices for rapid reloads.
  • Switchable embeddings: MedCPT on GPU with cross-encoder re-ranking, or OpenAI Ada-002 when running CPU-only.
  • LLM orchestration across GPT-4.1/4o and local Ollama models, with per-query JSONL + CSV logs.
  • Evaluation toolkit measuring ROUGE, BLEU, Context Precision/Recall, and Faithfulness on curated QA sets.

C-Risq

Graph-Attentive Multi-Omics Risk Stratification

C-Risq builds patient-specific gene networks from 430 TCGA-HNSCC samples, stacking 17-dimensional multi-omics features per gene into graph attention autoencoders. The system compresses each patient into a single latent manifold while preserving survival-critical interactions.

Key Highlights

  • Integrates expression, CNA, six-region methylation, and mutation signatures into unified 17D node embeddings.
  • GATv2 encoder-decoder reconstructs node/edge weights with ≥0.8 cosine fidelity, enabling interpretable latent space.
  • Attention analysis surfaces 70 high-salience genes and biomarker edges that distinguish survival cohorts.
  • Risk stratification models reach multiclass AUC >0.90 and binary AUC ≈0.98 for high vs lower risk groups.

Graph Attention Workflow

Patient Graph Builder

OmicsConfig pipelines assemble patient-specific adjacency matrices; edge weights encode pathway proximity and co-expression.

Attention Mining

GATv2 attention weights are harvested via Attention_Extracter.py to rank gene-gene interactions.

Latent Embedding

2,784 latent scalars per patient drive K-means clustering and downstream survival modeling pipelines.

Clinical Translation

GraphAnalysis toolkit links attention-derived signatures to Kaplanâ€“Meier separations and biomarker panels.
